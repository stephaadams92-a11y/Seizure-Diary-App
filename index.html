🆘 Seizure First Aid
1Stay calm. Note the time the seizure started.
2Clear the area — move hard or sharp objects away.
3Cushion the head with something soft.
4Do NOT restrain movements. Do NOT put anything in the mouth.
5Gently turn onto their side (recovery position) once convulsions stop.
6Stay with them until they are fully conscious and aware.
999Call 999 if: seizure >5 min, injury, doesn't recover, second seizure, or first ever.
